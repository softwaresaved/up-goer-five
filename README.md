# Explaining Work with Computers to Find Things Out

We use our brains to control computers. We want to explain what we do. We want to explain to people that do not work with computers, as well as to people that work with computers in a different way to us. This is important to us, as it helps people understand what we do and why. This allows people to ask questions, and makes us better at our jobs? Computer jobs are changing; this conversation should be happen!
